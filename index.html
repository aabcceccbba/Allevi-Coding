About Us

We have dataset for 3D printing information which comes from our products to show the usage and also some of the parameters of our products.

The dataset mainly includes three sessions: user information, print information, and print data. For the user information part, we have ID assigned to our customer and their email address for contact. For the print information, we provide files, pressure, crossinglinking, resolution, and wellplate parameters. For the print data, we provide live percentage, elasticity, and dead percentage parameters.
